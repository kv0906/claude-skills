# Will Writing Templates

## Template 1: AI Education Post

**Use when:** Explaining technical concepts, AI terms, technology definitions

```
[Term] là gì? Cụm từ rất hay được nhắc tới

[Term] là [1-sentence clear definition]

**Hình dung qua ví dụ [everyday thing]**
• **[Analogy element 1]**: Hãy tưởng tượng [scenario]...
• **[Analogy element 2]**: [Continue analogy]...
• **[Analogy element 3]**: [Complete picture]...

**Tại sao [Term] thú vị?**
1. **[Benefit/aspect 1]**
   [2-3 sentence explanation]
2. **[Benefit/aspect 2]**
   [2-3 sentence explanation]
3. **[Benefit/aspect 3]**
   [2-3 sentence explanation]

**Mấu chốt để hiểu [Term]:**
• [Key insight 1]
• [Key insight 2]

**Tóm lại**, [synthesis in 2-3 sentences connecting back to practical understanding]

#AI #[TopicTag]
```

### Key Elements:
- Opens with "là gì?" question
- Uses everyday analogy (thư viện, nhà hàng, etc.)
- Numbered benefits section
- "Mấu chốt" summary
- "Tóm lại" synthesis
- Hashtags at end

## Template 2: Philosophy/Life Post

**Use when:** Sharing wisdom quotes, life reflections, personal insights

```
Somewhere on the internet

————————————

"[Wisdom quote - can be Vietnamese or English]"

Mình vô tình thấy câu này, đầu lập tức nhảy số và connect nên viết vài dòng.

[Personal reflection on the quote - what it means to you]

[Expand on the wisdom - connect to broader truth]

Bản thân mình cũng thế, [vulnerability admission - share personal struggle related to this wisdom]...

[Universal truth derived - what we all can learn]

「[Optional reinforcing quote]」
— [Attribution]

#Life #Wisdom
```

### Key Elements:
- "Somewhere on the internet" opener
- Horizontal divider (————)
- Personal reaction ("đầu nhảy số")
- Vulnerability moment
- Universal truth extraction
- Optional closing blockquote
- #Life #Wisdom tags

## Template 3: News Analysis Post

**Use when:** Reacting to tech news, sharing links with commentary

### Short Version:
```
[News summary in 1-2 sentences]

[Personal reaction/question]

[Link]

#AI #[TopicTag]
```

### Long Version:
```
[Link]

[News summary in 2-3 bullet points]
• [Key point 1]
• [Key point 2]
• [Key point 3]

Đúng với câu nói:

"[Relevant quote that frames the news]"

[Personal analysis - why this matters]

[What this means for ae]

[Prediction for future / implications]

Think about it

#AI #[TopicTag]
```

### Key Elements:
- Link first (or news summary first)
- Bullet point summary
- Quote framing
- Personal take (never just share news)
- "Think about it" closer
- Relevant hashtags

## Template 4: Series/Educational Deep Dive

**Use when:** Part of ongoing series, comprehensive explanation

```
Tiếp tục series [Series Name]...

**[Main Topic]**

[Introduction connecting to previous posts]

Hãy thử tưởng tượng [analogy setup]...

[Build the analogy]

**[Section 1 Header]**

[Explanation with examples]

**[Section 2 Header]**

[Explanation with examples]

**[Section 3 Header]**

[Explanation with examples]

Điều này gợi nhớ đến [cross-domain connection]...

[Draw parallel to other field]

**Góc nhìn cá nhân:**

[Personal synthesis and opinion]

「[Closing wisdom quote]」
```

### Key Elements:

- Multiple bold sections
- Cross-domain connection
- "Góc nhìn cá nhân" personal take
- Closing quote
- Series hashtag

## Quick Reference: Post Type Selection

| Input Type | Template | Key Identifier |
|------------|----------|----------------|
| "X là gì?" | AI Education | Technical definition needed |
| Wisdom quote | Philosophy/Life | "Somewhere on the internet" |
| Tech news link | News Analysis | Link + commentary |
| Complex topic | Series/Deep Dive | Multiple sections |
| Personal reflection | Philosophy/Life | "Mình vô tình thấy..." |

## Length Guidelines

- **Short posts:** 50-200 chars - Quick thoughts, reactions
- **Medium posts:** 200-800 chars - Single concept
- **Long posts:** 800-3000+ chars - Educational deep dives
